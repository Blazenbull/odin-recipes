Beef Burger Recipe

Description

An easy to follow recipe for a succulent juicy burger made out of ground beef.

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup fine dry bread crumbs
  • A little bit of oil (sunflower or olive, your choice)
  • 4 leaves of lettuce (more if you want more vegatables)
  • 1 medium-sized white onion
  • 1 medium sized tomato
  • 4 burger buns
  • Medium sized bowl
  • Fork
  • Grill or Oven
  • Tray
  • Plates

Steps

  1. Preheat your grill or oven for high heat and lightly oil the grate or tray.
  2. Crack the egg and whisk it with the salt and pepper in the medium sized bowl. Whisk till it forms a consistent liquid.
  3. Add the ground beef and bread crumbs to the medium bowl and mix them well with the egg liquid.
  4. Form the mixture into four equal sized patties.
  5. Place the patties into the grill/oven for 6 to 8 minutes per side, longer if you wish for it to be cooked more thoroughly.
  6. Slice the burger buns in half while waiting for the patties to cook.
  7. Also slice the tomato, onions and lettuce to 4 slices while waiting for the patties to cook.
  8. Once patties cooked, place each patty together with tomato, onions and lettuce for serving.
